Strawberry Glazed Pie

Description

This is a restaurant-style strawberry glazed pie. Fresh strawberries are tossed with a gelatin glaze, and then chilled in a graham cracker crust. Serve topped with mounds of fluffy whipped cream.

Ingredents

  • 6 cups sliced fresh strawberries
  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 tablespoons strawberry flavored Jell-O
  • 4 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust

Steps

  1. Mix sugar, water, gelatin, and cornstarch together in a medium large saucepan. Boil for one minute, then remove from heat.
  2. Fold in sliced strawberries, toss until well coated.
  3. Pour into prepared graham cracker crust.
  4. Chill until well set, about 2 to 4 hours. Serve topped with whipped cream.
